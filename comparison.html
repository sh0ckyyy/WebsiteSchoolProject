Comparison

iPhone 8 Plus vs iPhone 13 Pro Max

Obviously everyone can say without thinking that iPhone 13 Pro Max is much better than iPhone 8 Plus. But you would be really surprised about a fact that these two can actually compete.


Mayor difference between these two is "unlocking" technology, where iPhone 8 Plus uses TouchID and iPhone 13 Pro Max FaceID. I tested both for a enough time to say that speed is similiar on both. Now it just depends on what do you prefer the more.


Battery life is very similiar on both phones, even tho iPhone 13 Pro Max comes with larger battery, it also has a much bigger screen. That causes more battery drain.
